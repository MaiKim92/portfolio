Bio

Ever since a young age, Kim has always aspired to be a software developer. However, life is always full of surprises. He graduated from Hanoi National University of Education and had two successful years as a teacher. But deep down inside, something was calling out to him. But once again, life is full of surprises - when he took the developer course at Funix, he knew it was his chance to fulfill his true dreams.

After four months of training, using the skills and knowledge accumulated from doing pet projects, Kim was ready to challenge a new realm. And so by October 2021, he became part of over 700 employees at SotaTek to fulfill his childhood dream.
